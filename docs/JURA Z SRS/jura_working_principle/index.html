Jura Z Series Coffee Machine: Working Principle and Crucial Elements

Working Principle​

  1. Selection of Coffee Type

    • When a coffee type (e.g., espresso, cappuccino, latte macchiato) is selected via the machine’s touchscreen or rotary dial, the pre-programmed recipe is activated:
      • Coffee strength (grind amount)
      • Water volume
      • Brewing time
      • Milk frothing or steaming settings (if applicable).

  2. Grinding Process

    • The built-in Aroma G3 grinder grinds the coffee beans to the required coarseness efficiently and quietly, preserving the flavor.

  3. Brewing Unit (Core of the Machine)

    • Freshly ground coffee is transferred to the brewing unit.
    • Pre-infusion occurs: hot water is sprayed over coffee grounds to release aroma using the Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.).
    • Studer launched a series of intensive tests, constantly varying the parameters and verifying his measured results in coffee tastings. ‘We force the water at short, precisely measured intervals through the ground coffee and, as a result, extract a maximum of aroma.’
    • Water is then forced through the coffee grounds under high pressure (around 15 bars).
    • The brewed coffee flows into the cup.

  4. Milk Frothing System (For Milk-Based Drinks)

    • For drinks like cappuccino or latte, the fine foam technology froths the milk.
    • Milk is drawn from an external container/tube, heated, and dispensed as silky microfoam into the cup.

  5. Water Heating and Delivery

    • The Thermoblock heating system rapidly heats water to the required temperature for brewing or steaming milk.
    • Hot water or steam is directed with precision for optimal results.

  6. Cleaning and Maintenance

    • The machine automatically rinses the brewing unit and milk system after brewing to maintain hygiene.

Crucial Elements​

Aroma G3 Grinder

  • Quick and precise grinding with minimal noise while preserving aroma.

Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.)

  • Maximizes flavor extraction for short specialties like espresso by pulsing hot water through the coffee grounds.

Thermoblock Heating System

  • Rapidly heats water to the ideal temperature for brewing or steaming milk.

Intelligent Pre-Brew Aroma System (I.P.B.A.S.)

  • Optimizes pre-infusion to release the coffee's full aroma.

Intelligent Water System (I.W.S.)

  • Works with the CLARIS Smart water filter to ensure clean water, reduce scale buildup, and improve coffee flavor.

Summary​

The Jura Z series automates the coffee-making process with systems like the Aroma G3 grinder and Pulse Extraction Process (P.E.P.) to ensure consistent, high-quality coffee. Key components include the grinder, brewing unit, milk system, and automatic cleaning mechanisms for a seamless and professional coffee experience.
